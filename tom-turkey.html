Legend of Tom Turkey


With his friends, a legend begins.
His name is Turkey Tom.
He roams the fields for enemies
To hunt them with great calm

The turkey gods did grant poor Tom
--with a magical skill.
He can scorch those who ate his folks.
Those gluttons he shall kill!

He fattens them up, gives them beer,
But for them it’s their end.
Unknowing and unwittingly,
So fat they cannot fend

Tom Turkey seeks out his revenge.
Tom Turkey never stops!
So, his friends do enjoy a feast.
Those savage beasts taste tops.

The ones that ate Tom Turkey’s kin
Have just met their grilled doom.
Once a festive table they laid,
Now a turkey belly's tomb

Happy Thanksgiving, everyone!
Love,
Eileen
